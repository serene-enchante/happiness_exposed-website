## Keeping a record

Every person carries a story that deserves to be written down. Sometimes it is a quiet habit, like a cup of tea at dawn. Other times it is a commitment, like showing up for friends when the work gets difficult.

One Day is a project designed to collect and share these threads of human connection.

## The inspiration

This platform is inspired by the simple texture of everyday life. By looking closer at the routines we repeat, the things we build, and the conversations we have, we find the sparks that make us who we are.

Thank you for being here, reading, and sharing your journey.
