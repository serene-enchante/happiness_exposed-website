**ALESHA LURIE, Founder/Owner of Alesha’s Leashes**

**‘It’s ‘Monday Morning’** and Alesha is greeted enthusiastically at the door of her client’s home by Maddy, the sweet little dog who lives there. Alesha started her business, ‘Alesha’s Leashes’ years ago in Arlington, Virginia. It’s a dog walking and pet house sitter service for owners who are away from home overnight or they are not able to walk their dog during the day. They just call Alesha.  

So where did this love for this type of work begin for Alesha?  Well she grew up with a big white Labrador dog named, “Sheba”.  “I loved that dog!” Alesha will passionately share as she remembers the wonderful endearing times she had with her dog while growing up. So today she has reignited that happy childhood feeling now that she has made it her sole work to spend her days interacting with dogs of her many local clients. And she has many clients!  She is typically booked for a year in advance and in high demand because her clients can see how much their pets love being with Alesha!

One special story about her strong connection with her dogs that she cares for is the endearing story of Axel! Axel’s owners decided that they would hire a dog sitter for their new puppy BEFORE he was brought home for the first time.  They met with Alesha and it was set up for her to be at their house when the little puppy arrived home. It was love at first sight for both of them and Alesha remained in contact with Axel and his owners even after they moved five states away when he was      years old.  It’s been over seven years and Axel loves his facetime visits with Alesha!  She even mails him toys, but first she sleeps with the toy one night so he can pick up on her scent. The owners told Alesha that Axel only licks his favorite toys and he licks every toy he receives from her! The owners even offered to pay for her flight to their new home when they travel and have to leave Axell for a trip. But, Alesha has to honor her commitments to her current scheduled clients.  Maybe one day, they will meet in person again! This is a true reflection on what doing what makes you happy is a gift that is so welcomed by the receivers.

Alesha claims wholeheartedly that her pet work is like a paid vacation every day!  Alesha is aligned with her true work that makes her happy and excited each time she cares for her client’s pets.
